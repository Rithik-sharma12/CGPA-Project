How CGPA is Calculated

CGPA =
Σ (Grade Points × Credits)
Σ Total Credits

Example:

Subject 1: Grade A (8 points) × 3 credits = 24 grade points

Subject 2: Grade B+ (7 points) × 4 credits = 28 grade points

Total Grade Points: 52, Total Credits: 7

CGPA = 52 ÷ 7 = 7.43

What is CGPA?

Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is a grading system used to evaluate your overall academic performance. It's calculated by taking the average of grade points obtained in all subjects, considering the credit hours of each subject.
